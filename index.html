Technology

    Technology encompasses the use of tools, techniques, and systems to solve problems and achieve objectives, impacting every aspect of modern life.

    Science

    Science is the pursuit of knowledge about the natural world through observation, experimentation, and theoretical explanation, forming the foundation of modern advancements.

    History

    History is the study of past events, examining how societies have changed over time and how these changes shape the present and future.

    Art

    Art is a creative expression through various forms such as painting, sculpture, music, and literature, reflecting cultural values and personal emotions.
